About us

TacoLulú

Cerveceria Y Taqueria “Taco LuLú” (named after the owner Michael’s dog, LuLú) is a cozy neighborhood Cerveceria Y Taqueria serving traditional Mexican food located in the heart of Chicago’s West Loop, just blocks from Willis Tower, Union Station and Old St. Pat’s Church!

We take pride in making our dishes from scratch daily, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. We are proud to feature “Arrachera”, which is a fine cut of inner skirt steak that is trimmed in-house and char-grilled fresh throughout the day. We also offer a full menu with home-style recipes including “pollo” marinated shredded chicken, house-made pastor & picadillo, delicious salsas, rice & beans, guacamole, and our addictive pico de gallo! We have a selection of gluten-free and vegetarian options as well!

We are Currently BYOB for the moment but will be adding beer, house made margaritas & sangria soon!

Our Passion is to Share Our Favorite Foods and Inviting Atmosphere with You, Your Family, and Friends. See you Soon!
