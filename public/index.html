ABOUT US

CERVECERIA & TAQUERIA

Taco LuLú is a cozy neighborhood Cerveceria Y Taqueria serving traditional Mexican food located in the heart of Chicago’s West Loop. We take pride in making our dishes from scratch daily, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. We are proud to feature “Arrachera”, which is a fine cut of inner skirt steak that is trimmed in house and Char-grilled fresh throughout the day. We also offer a full menu with home-style recipes including “Pollo” Marinated Chicken, Char-Grilled Chicken Breast, House made Chorizo & Pastor, Red & Green Salsas, Rice & Beans, Guacamole, and our addictive Pico de Gallo! In addition, we have a selection of Gluten-Free and Vegetarian options as well. We are currently BYOB for the moment but will be adding beer, House made Margaritas & Sangria soon!! Our passion is to share our favorite foods and inviting atmosphere with you, your family, and friends. We look forward to meeting you and seeing you satisfied and smiling as you indulge in our favorite dishes. See you Soon!
