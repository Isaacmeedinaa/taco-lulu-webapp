ABOUT US

CERVECERIA & TAQUERIA

Cerveceria Y Taqueria “Taco LuLú” (named after the owner Michael’s dog, LuLú) is a cozy neighborhood Cerveceria Y Taqueria serving traditional Mexican food located in the heart of Chicago’s West Loop, just blocks from Willis Tower, Union Station and Old St. Pat’s Church!

We take pride in making our dishes from scratch daily, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.

We offer a selection of ice cold beer, mixed drinks and house made Margaritas!

Our Passion is to Share Our Favorite Foods and Inviting Atmosphere with You, Your Family, and Friends.

Hope to see you soon!

Michael & LuLú

MEXICAN DISHES

We are proud to feature “Arrachera”, which is a fine cut of inner skirt steak that is trimmed in house and Char-grilled fresh throughout the day. We also offer a full menu with home-style recipes including “Pollo” Marinated Chicken, Char-Grilled Chicken Breast, House made Chorizo & Pastor, Red & Green Salsas, Rice & Beans, Guacamole, and our addictive Pico de Gallo! In addition, we have a selection of Gluten-Free and Vegetarian options as well.
